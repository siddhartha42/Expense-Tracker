# Universal Expense & Investment Tracker (Excel)

A comprehensive and adaptable personal finance tool designed for anyone who wants to track their daily expenses and investment activities in one place. This Excel workbook offers:

- **Flexible Date Ranges:** Track any period—week, month, year, or custom—just by changing two cells.
- **Unlimited Expense Entries:** Log multiple transactions per day with 500+ rows available.
- **Dedicated Investment Tracking:** Separate sheet for tracking all types of investments with full analytics.
- **Auto-calculated Summaries:** Get instant insights, category breakdowns, daily averages, highest expense, investment analysis, and more.
- **Visual Dashboards:** Instantly updating pie and bar charts for both spending and investing.
- **Budget Planning:** Set monthly budgets for each expense category and investment targets.
- **Simple Setup:** No coding or addons needed; everything works out-of-the-box in Excel.
- **Customizable:** Easily edit, add new categories, and personalize formulas or layout.

## Sheets Overview

- **Setup:** Set your tracking dates (Start Date, End Date).
- **Daily Expenses:** Enter expenses—date, category, description, amount, payment method, notes.
- **Investments:** Log investments—date, type, description, amount, duration/target, expected return, notes.
- **Summary & Insights:** Automated analysis dashboard of both expenses and investments.
- **Charts & Graphs:** Pie charts and bar charts for visual insights.
- **Budget Plan:** Set and monitor budget limits for each category.
- **Investment Plan:** Track investment targets and progress.
- **Instructions:** Built-in step-by-step guide.

## How to Use

1. **Open the workbook in Excel.**
2. Go to the **Setup** sheet. Enter your desired Start Date and End Date (format: YYYY-MM-DD).
3. Log expenses in **Daily Expenses** (multiple rows allowed per day).
4. Track all investments in the **Investments** sheet.
5. Review your financial health on the **Summary & Insights** and **Charts & Graphs** pages.
6. Set your budgets and investment goals in **Budget Plan** and **Investment Plan.**
7. Refer to the **Instructions** sheet for help and tips.

## Features

- Supports unlimited tracking duration—from weeks to multiple years.
- Handles multiple transactions and investments per day.
- Customizable categories and investment types.
- Fully formula-driven—no external dependencies needed.
- Colorful visual charts for easy analysis.
- Works offline. Excel required.
